How to Check WBCHSE HS Result 2025

The West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE) is expected to release the Class 12 results in May 2025.

Steps to Check the Result:

  1. Visit the official result website: wbresults.nic.in
  2. Click on the “Higher Secondary Result 2025” link.
  3. Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth (DOB).
  4. Click on "Submit" to view your marks.
  5. Download or print your result for future reference.

Always use the official website for authentic information.
